Sacred Origin

Rooted in the
Eternal Tradition

Aham Brahmaasmi Foundation® (ABF) is a registered public charitable trust dedicated to the spiritual advancement of earnest seekers worldwide. Rooted in the sacred town of Sringeri, Karnataka, ABF operates under the divine blessings of the Jagadgurus of Dakshinamnaya Sri Sharada Peetham.

"In today's world, despite unprecedented technological advancement, many of us yearn for deeper meaning and lasting fulfilment — Aham Brahmaasmi addresses these through the Shastras and the Sanatana Parampara."

Through thoughtfully designed courses, guided Shravana sessions, and immersive Vedanta retreats, ABF supports seekers in cultivating discernment — allowing understanding to mature into a quiet, abiding inner presence.

Presiding Deity

Bhagavati Sharadamba

ಶೃಂಗೇರಿ ಶ್ರೀ ಶಾರದಾಂಬೆ

All activities of Aham Brahmaasmi Foundation are conducted under the grace and divine blessings of Bhagavati Sharadamba of Sringeri and the Acharya Parampara — the living embodiment of Saraswati, Goddess of Knowledge and Liberation.

Signature Programmes

01 🕯️

Samskara Sudha

A seven-day immersive programme for children and youth, instilling cultural, spiritual, and moral values through Puja training, Sanskrit learning, and guidance from eminent scholars of the Sringeri tradition.

Children & Youth
02 📿

Vedanta Shravana Series

Online discourse series in eight languages based on the commentary of Jagadguru Sri Adi Shankara Bhagavatpadacharya. Structured for both beginners and advanced seekers, led by esteemed scholars worldwide.

Online · 8 Languages
03 🏔️

Immersive Vedanta Retreats

Multi-day residential retreats held in sacred locations including Sringeri, Kalady, and internationally in Melbourne, Sydney, Singapore, Houston, and more — offering deep, uninterrupted Vedantic inquiry.

India & International

Structured Online Courses

Carefully curated learning pathways covering Upanishads, Bhagavad Gita, Vivekachudamani, and key Prakarana Granthas — designed for seekers to study within a living lineage rooted in authentic tradition.

Self-Paced & Live

Community Satsangas

Pan-India · Global

Purpose of This Page

॥ श्री शारदाम्बा प्रसन्ना ॥

This website is a specimen of AI-assisted front-end web design, prepared by Sourav Karanth as part of his application to Aham Brahmaasmi Foundation. It demonstrates, in working form, his ability to design and deploy visually compelling, spiritually faithful, and functionally sound digital experiences for ABF's mission.

Every element of this page — the typographic hierarchy using classical serif fonts, the warm gold-saffron-crimson palette drawn from the temple aesthetic, the animated mandala elements, the programme cards, the multilingual language wheel, and the global reach section — was conceived and built using AI-assisted design workflows as described in the letter of application dated 30th May 2026.

The two photographs embedded in this page — the sacred Sringeri temple and Bhagavati Sharadamba — are images personally provided by the applicant, symbolising his living connection to the Sringeri tradition. The content is drawn from publicly available information about ABF's actual programmes, accurately representing the Foundation's eight-language outreach, global reach, and flagship initiatives such as Samskara Sudha.

This specimen proves that Sourav Karanth can independently produce production-grade digital assets for ABF — including event landing pages, programme registration pages, outreach microsites, and digital content — without requiring a professional developer for routine tasks, exactly as stated in his application.
